# ADR-031 — What is the required AI agent change process?

## Status

Confirmed

## Area

AI Delivery

## Decision

Every change must begin from a scoped task, reference relevant ADRs, include code/tests/migrations/docs, run required checks, and produce a decision request rather than making unapproved product or architecture choices.

## Rationale

Creates repeatable AI-assisted delivery.

## Consequences and trade-offs

Adds process overhead but reduces rework and hidden decisions.
